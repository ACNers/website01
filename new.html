ABOUT US

Amrita CyberNation is an annual event dedicated to promoting cyber awareness and education among students. Held every October in conjunction with Cyber Awareness Month, our event aims to foster a deeper understanding of cybersecurity through a series of engaging and educational activities.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower students with the knowledge and skills necessary to navigate the digital world safely and securely. By providing a platform for hands-on learning and real-world experience, we strive to create a community of informed and proactive individuals ready to tackle the challenges of cybersecurity.
