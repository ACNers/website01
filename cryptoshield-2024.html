WHAT IS

CRYPTOSHIELD

CryptoShield Hackathon'24 is designed to ignite student passion for innovation and problem-solving. This event provides a unique platform for students to collaborate, brainstorm, and build incredible tech solutions. The Grand Finale will be held offline at Amrita School of Engineering, Chennai, where the full team, along with an optional mentor, must be physically present throughout the hackathon.

ELIGIBILITY

Open to all students currently enrolled in College or University programs such as BE/B.Tech/Diploma, MCA/BCA, ME/M.Tech, B.Sc./M.Sc., as well as BBA/MBA.
